J.C. Pompe medal

  • September 2012
  • Design Mirjam Mieras
  • Material Ebony

A great design by Mirjam Mieras. I recommended ebony for this project: it is stable, heavy and hard as rock yet creamy as butter, it feels very nice in your hands and needs no finish. The medal slides perfectly into the ebony.
